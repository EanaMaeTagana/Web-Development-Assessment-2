Our Story

Welcome to Cherry Picked, where our love for vintage fashion meets a dedication to timeless style. Founded by a group of passionate individuals with a shared vision, Cherry Picked was born from a desire to celebrate the artistry and elegance of bygone eras.


Our journey began with a simple belief: that every piece of clothing has a story to tell. With this in mind, we set out to curate a collection that honors the beauty and craftsmanship of vintage fashion while also embracing sustainability. Each item in our store is hand-selected for its quality, authenticity, and unique charm.


As we continue to grow, our commitment to providing exceptional service and fostering a community of vintage enthusiasts remains unwavering.
